About us

Sweetify the Workshop

At Sweetify, we are a student run cookie workshop where creativity and good vibes mix with the smell of fresh dough. We started from a simple idea and turned it into a place where we experiment with flavors, learn together, and have fun while working toward a meaningful goal. Every cookie we make carries our energy: youthful, curious, and always ready to try something new. More than a workshop, this is a community built on teamwork, kindness, and the joy of creating something sweet for a good cause. Welcome in and enjoy the journey with us.
